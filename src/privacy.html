Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 9/08/2025

WhereFam (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

1. No Personal Data Collected or Stored

We do not collect, store, or process any personal data on our servers. Our app operates on a peer-to-peer (P2P) model, meaning all communications and location sharing occur directly between users' devices. We do not have access to the content you share or your location data.

2. Peer-to-Peer Communication

All location sharing and communication are handled device-to-device using a decentralized P2P network. This means:

  • Your location data stays on your device and is sent only to selected recipients.
  • No data is transmitted to or stored on any centralized server we control.
  • We cannot access, intercept, or log your location or messages.

3. Donations and Payments (via RevenueCat)

We use RevenueCat, a third-party service, to process voluntary donations or tips. We do not collect or store any payment information ourselves. RevenueCat may collect data such as:

  • Purchase history (e.g., donation amount, time of donation)
  • Device identifiers (e.g., to manage subscriptions or restore purchases)

Please refer to RevenueCat’s Privacy Policy for details on how they handle data.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we’ll notify you via the app or update the “Effective Date” above. Continued use of the app means you agree to the revised policy.
